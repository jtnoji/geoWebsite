§2 — SAMPLING

Ten runs, not one screenshot

AI answers change between runs — a single fetch is a coin flip.1 We run every query 10× per engine and report the rate. A single-run "AI rank" is just noise.

1. Repeat runs turn over 65–81% of results. SE Ranking, 5,000-keyword study, 2025.
